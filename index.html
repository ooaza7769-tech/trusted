What Trusted Devs is

Trusted Devs is a community built for experienced Roblox developers, the people who actually ship games, not just talk about them. Every member has been vetted for real, demonstrable work: shipped titles, production-quality scripts, or a portfolio that holds up.

On the other side of the table are investors and studios looking to fund or staff their next Roblox title. Instead of sorting through anonymous applicants, they come to Trusted Devs to find developers who are already verified, and developers come here to find work that's actually worth their time.

Everything happens inside our Discord: portfolio reviews, hiring calls, project pitches, and the day-to-day conversation between the people building Roblox's next generation of games.

Why developers and investors trust us

  • Manual vetting, every time
    Every developer is reviewed by our management team before they get the verified role, no exceptions.
  • Real portfolios, real games
    We look at shipped work, not just claims. If it's on the profile, it's been checked.
  • Direct line to investors
    Studios and investors post real opportunities directly to the server, no recruiters in between.
  • A community, not a marketplace
    Developers help each other, share resources, and grow together, hiring is a byproduct of a real community.

How it works

Two sides, one server. Here's what each path looks like once you're in.

For developers

Get verified, get found

Bring your portfolio, get vetted, and start getting seen by the studios and investors already in the server.

  1. Join the Discord and introduce yourself with your portfolio or past work.
  2. Get reviewed by the management team for a verified developer role.
  3. Get matched with projects, teams, and investors looking for your skillset.
For investors & studios

Find developers you can trust

Skip the cold DMs and unverified applicants. Post your project and reach developers who've already been checked out.

  1. Join the Discord and share what you're building and who you need.
  2. Browse verified developers by specialty: scripting, building, UI, and more.
  3. Hire directly, with the Trusted Devs badge as your baseline for quality.

The vetting process

The "Trusted" in Trusted Devs isn't a name, it's a bar every member has to clear.

STEP 01

Apply

Share your portfolio, shipped games, or scripting samples in the server.

STEP 02

Review

Our management team checks your work for quality and authenticity.

STEP 03

Verify

Approved developers receive the verified badge and full server access.

STEP 04

Connect

Get visible to investors and studios actively hiring in the server.
